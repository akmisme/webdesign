Our Tea Service

Tea, the timeless beverage enjoyed by cultures around the world, has a fascinating history and an array of flavors that cater to diverse palates. Every Sunday, our cafe celebrates and honors our beloved senior community by offering them a free cup of tea as a small token of appreciation. This special gesture is our way of creating a warm and inviting space for older guests to relax, connect, and enjoy a peaceful moment. It's the perfect opportunity to show gratitude and spread joy to those who bring so much wisdom and charm to our world.
